Meet Jenna

I'm Not Just Your Photographer —
I'm Your Biggest Hype Girl

I believe God writes beautiful love stories, and your photo session should reflect that. No stiff poses, no awkward silences — just the two of you, being completely yourselves, while I capture the real moments that matter.

Whether you're newly engaged, celebrating an anniversary, or saying "I do" — I'm here to make you feel confident, comfortable, and completely cared for. I'll hype you up, make you laugh (dad jokes included), and deliver a gallery that makes you fall in love all over again.

Proudly Michigan-made. I was born and raised in the Great Lakes State, and there's nowhere I'd rather build my business. Michigan gives us four breathtaking seasons, endless lakeshore backdrops, and sunsets that paint the sky over Lake Michigan in ways that make my camera weep with joy. Whether we're shooting among the dunes of Sleeping Bear, beneath a canopy of autumn maples, or in a cozy downtown café in Grand Rapids — this state is part of your story, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

Every love story is a gift from above — and it's my joy to help you tell yours. Based in [City], Michigan — available for travel worldwide.

Got Questions?

The Couple Session Q&A

We're not models — are you sure this is for us?

Absolutely! Most of my couples have never had professional photos taken before. That's my specialty — making you feel completely at ease, guiding you into flattering poses, and capturing genuine moments. God created you beautifully, and my job is simply to let that shine through!

How many images will we receive and when?

Depending on your session length, you'll receive 50–75+ fully edited high-resolution images. Sneak peeks land in your inbox within 72 hours, and the full gallery is delivered within 3–4 weeks.

What's your editing style?

My style is bright, warm, and true-to-life with a touch of editorial polish. I focus on clean skin tones, vibrant colors, and natural light — photos that feel timeless, not trendy.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! I totally understand that life gets expensive. Payment plans are available for all packages — just ask when you inquire. I never want cost to be the reason you miss out on documenting the love story God gave you.

What if it rains on our session day?

No worries! I keep a close eye on the forecast and we'll reschedule if needed — no extra charge. Some of my favorite sessions have happened on overcast days with the most beautiful soft light. Even the weather is in His hands!

Do you travel for sessions?

Yes! I'm based in [City], Michigan and include travel within a 30-mile radius. For destinations further afield, I'm happy to provide a custom travel quote. Adventure sessions are my favorite!
